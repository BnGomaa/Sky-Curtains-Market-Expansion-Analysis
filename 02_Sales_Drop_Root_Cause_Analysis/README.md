# Sales Drop Root Cause Analysis

## Objective
Investigate the 16% month-over-month sales decline (April to May) and identify the primary drivers behind the drop.

## Key Findings
- Total sales declined by 16% (-222K).
- The decline was concentrated in:
  - Alexandria (-57.6%)
  - Cairo (-53.8%)
  - Tanta (-44.5%)
- The Standard product category dropped by -54.5% (-235K), exceeding the total net loss.
- Both New and Returning customers were affected, indicating a product- and region-driven issue.

## Conclusion
The Standard product category represents a **single point of failure** for this period and is the primary root cause of the sales decline.
